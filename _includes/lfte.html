From the Editor:

Welcome to the online home of Tales of Murder Press, LLC, an independent publisher of classic & out-of-print murder mystery and crime short stories, novellas, novelettes, and novels.

If it's a story that involves murder or violent, unnatural death in any component, or involves crime and detectives, whether professional or amateur, we publish it.

My job, as editor, is to search out and produce the best possible collection of short stories and novels for your reading pleasure.

The job is simple:

  • Search the back rooms, basements & attics around the country for old detective and mystery magazines, books and other collections.
  • Scan, edit, revise and turn into web-readable stories.
